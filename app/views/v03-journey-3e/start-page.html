Tell DWP about a change

You can tell us about a change for some of your benefits using this service but not all of them.

This is because not all of your benefits are on this service yet.

It’s important that you tell us about changes as soon as they happen. Any delay may mean you receive too much money and will have to make a repayment.

When you tell us about your change we may ask you some other questions about your circumstances. This is to check you are getting the right amount of benefits.

You may be asked to provide evidence, for example a driving licence, bank statement or payslip. You will have 28 days to provide this evidence.
