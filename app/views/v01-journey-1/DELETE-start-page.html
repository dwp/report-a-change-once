You can tell DWP about a change for benefits using this service but not all of them. This is because not all of your benefits support reporting a change online yet.

How to use Report A Change

You'll need to answer some questions and tell us about your change.

Before you use Report A Change

You may need to provide supporting evidence depending on the change you need to tell us about. We'll let you know what you need to provide, it could be things like:

  • Your most recent council tax bill
  • Your most recent utility bill
  • Your current tenancy agreement

Use Report A Change

{{ govukButton({ text: "Start now", href: "account-home.html", isStartButton: true }) }}

Organisations Report A Change will contact

Report A Change will notify:

  • Attendance Allowance
  • Carers Allowance
  • Disability Living Allowance
  • Pension Credit

If you receive a benefit that is not listed above you must let the relevant organisations know about your change of circumstances.

After you use Report A Change

You will receive confirmation that DWP has received your change request and will inform the benefits you recieve of your change in one go.

If you do not use Report A Change

You must let the relevant organisations know about your change of circumstances.
