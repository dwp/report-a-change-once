Providing evidence

When you report a change using ‘Tell DWP about a change’, you may be asked to provide evidence.

The evidence you need to provide will depend on the type of change you are reporting.

Evidence types

Address

  • local authority
  • council tax bill
  • driving licence
  • utility bill
  • tenancy agreement
  • mortgage statement

Capital

  • bank statements
  • annual statements

Education

  • student loan documents
  • educational institute documents

Earnings (employed and self-employed)

  • payslips
  • bank statements
  • pension statements
  • P60
  • self-employed accounts
