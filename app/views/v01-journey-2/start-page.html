Tell DWP about a change of address

You can tell us about a change of address for some of your benefits using this service but not all of them.

This is because not all of your benefits are online yet.

You cannot use this service to update your address for some of your benefits

Universal Credit

You can update your address using your Universal Credit online account if you have one or contact the Universal Credit helpline

Personal Independence Payment

You can update your address by calling the Disability Service Centre

You can use this service to update your address for the following benefits

If you continue, you can update your address online for:

  • Attendance Allowance
  • Carer's Allowance

To change your address for any other benefit or State Pension, you must contact the relevant team directly.
