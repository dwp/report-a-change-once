Report a change once

Report a change once is an online service that lets you report a change in circumstances to the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) in one place.

This is a new service so some benefits and types of circumstances are not online yet.

You can use this service for the following benefits

If you continue, you can tell us about a change of circumstances online for:

  • Attendance Allowance
  • Carer's Allowance

You cannot use this service for some of your benefits

Universal Credit

You can update your address using your Universal Credit online account if you have one or contact the Universal Credit helpline

Personal Independence Payment

You can update your address by calling the Disability Service Centre
