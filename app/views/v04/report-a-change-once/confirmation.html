What happens next

Your change will be sent to the relevant benefit office. It can take up to 7 days to process.

Your new home address will be shown on the personal details page when it is processed.

Tell us about changes for your other benefits

To report changes to Pension Credit you need to contact the benefit team directly.

Find their contact details in a letter they have sent to you, or in guidance about the benefit on GOV.UK (opens in a new tab).
